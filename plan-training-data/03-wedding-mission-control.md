## Plan

### T-9 → T-7 — ignition sequence
- [x] 1. venue booked: botanical garden, rain backup included (effort: L)
- [x] 2. guest list v1 162 → frozen at 140 after The Great Aunt Debate (effort: M)
- [~] 3. photographer: two finalists, decide by Friday (effort: S)
- [ ] 4. caterer tasting #1 — must be venue-approved (effort: M) needs: 1
- 🎉 MILESTONE: venue signed — the date is real now, tell everyone

### T-6 → T-4 — systems assembly
- [ ] 5. save-the-dates out (effort: S) needs: 2
- [ ] 6. band vs DJ decision — band books out 6 months ahead, decide by T-6 or lose them (effort: S)
- [ ] 7. outfit fittings round 1 of 3 — aerospace-grade tailoring tolerances (effort: M)
- [ ] 8. registry live + hotel room blocks for out-of-town crew (effort: S)

### T-3 → T-1 months — final countdown checks
- [ ] 9. invitations at exactly T-10 weeks; RSVPs tracked in shared sheet (effort: M) needs: 5
- [ ] 10. menu locked after tasting #2 (effort: M) needs: 4
- [ ] 11. seating chart v1 — the two cricket-argument uncles get different hemispheres (effort: M) needs: 9
- [ ] 12. marriage license — 90-day validity window; not early, not late (effort: S)
- [ ] 13. day-of run sheet: every vendor, phone number, minute from 7am–midnight (effort: L) needs: 10,11
- 🎉 MILESTONE: RSVP count final; caterer gets the number; deep breath

### T-1 week — terminal count
- [ ] 14. confirm every vendor by CALL, not text (effort: S) needs: 13
- [ ] 15. run sheet handed to day-of coordinator — we are passengers now (effort: S) needs: 13
- [ ] 16. break in the shoes (both humans) + emergency kit: needle, stain pen, backup ring, granola bars (effort: S)

### T-0 — liftoff
- [ ] 17. eat breakfast (mission-critical; history says it gets skipped)
- [ ] 18. phones off at 3pm; get married
- 🎉 MILESTONE: mission accomplished — all anomalies are somebody else's problem until Monday

⚠️ RISK: license validity is a 90-day window — booking the appointment early actually voids the plan.
⚠️ RISK: April rain — venue's covered pavilion is confirmed in writing, re-verify at T-1 month.
